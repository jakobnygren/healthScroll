We live in a world of artificial light, synthetic hormones, and mimicked signals. To awaken the original endocrine code, we must:

- Support and restore the **pineal gland** and its melatonin output.

- Heal the **thyroid-adrenal axis**.
- Normalize **insulin** and **leptin** signaling (key for fat storage and hunger).
- Detox endocrine disruptors (e.g. plastics, pesticides, soy derivatives).
- Rebuild **dopamine** tone and **light sensitivity** via morning sun and magnetism.
